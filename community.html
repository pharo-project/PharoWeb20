Community & help

Discord team

We hang out on Discord (Invitation at https://discord.gg/QewZMZa), where we discuss everything for Pharo users and developers.

You can get an instant invite following this link.

Q&A Community

If you have any questions regarding Pharo, do not hesitate to post in the Pharo mailing lists or on Discord Team (Invitation at https://discord.gg/QewZMZa). You may use StackOverflow but we do not use it much and prefer the mailing list or discord. Be sure to tag your question with the pharo tag, so that your question is not lost amongst all the questions on StackOverflow.
