LawTracker

Lawsuit tracker is a desktop application to follow trials and do reporting/liquidation of them. It has a customer-designed DSL for querying and it exports data to PDF and CSV.
Lawsuit tracker is actively used daisly by around 20 users. In its 5 years of usage it has not need maintenance more than two times. Lawsuit Tracker has been developed using Pharo, Glamour, Magritte and Voyage.
